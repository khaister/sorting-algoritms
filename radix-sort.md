# Radix Sort

> __Time Complexity__: O(_kn_) (average/worst), where _k_ is the max number of digits of the input elements

```txt
IN                      OUT
          .      .      .
329     720     720     329
457     355     329     355
657     436     436     436
839 ==> 457 ==> 839 ==> 457
436     657     355     657
720     329     457     720
355     839     657     839
```
